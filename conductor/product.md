# Product Guide - Food & Water Tracker

## Initial Concept
An application for diet and health tracking that allows users to monitor meals, nutrients, water intake, and daily progress. It aims to transform nutritional management through a complete and intuitive interface accessible on both web and mobile (Android).

## Target Users
- **Health-conscious individuals:** People focused on tracking daily nutrition and hydration to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
- **Organized Shoppers:** Users who want to streamline their grocery shopping by linking it to their dietary requirements and plans.

## Core Goals
- **Frictionless Logging:** Provide a minimalist and functional interface where users can log meals and water with minimal effort and taps.
- **Sustained Engagement:** Motivate users through a multi-layered gamification system, including daily streaks, milestone badges, and experience points (XP).
- **Seamless Portability:** Ensure a consistent and high-quality experience across web and Android platforms using Capacitor.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive Meal Management:** Searchable/filterable food database, custom meal creation, and automatic nutrient calculation.
- **Interactive Daily Diary:** Real-time tracking of nutritional goals with dynamic progress bars and historical data access.
- **Advanced Hydration Tracking:** Custom daily targets, quick-add logging, and personalized reminders.
- **Integrated Shopping Lists:** Automatic list generation from meal plans combined with manual entry for household essentials.
- **Gamification System:**
    - **Daily Streaks:** Rewards for consistent logging of nutrition and water.
    - **Milestone Badges:** Recognition for long-term achievements (e.g., "30-day streak", "100L water").
    - **Progression System:** Earn XP for healthy actions to reach higher levels.
- **Visual Analytics:** Detailed charts (pie and line) for analyzing macronutrient distribution and caloric trends.

## Aesthetic & UX Vision
- **Minimalist & Functional:** The design prioritizes speed, efficiency, and clarity. The interface remains clean and uncluttered, ensuring that data entry is never a chore and that insights are immediately visible.
